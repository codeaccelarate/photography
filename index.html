Frequently Asked Questions

For landscape photography, it's recommended to use a camera with a wide-angle lens and high resolution, such as a DSLR or a mirrorless camera with a full-frame sensor.

To improve your photography skills, practice regularly, experiment with different techniques, study composition principles, and learn from feedback and critiques.

For low light photography, consider using a wide aperture (low f-stop number), slow shutter speed, and higher ISO settings. Additionally, using a tripod can help stabilize the camera for longer exposures.
